What is Sentinel-2?

Sentinel-2 is an Earth-observation satellite mission under the Copernicus Programme, operated by the European Space Agency (ESA).

It captures high-resolution, multispectral images of the Earth’s land surface to monitor agriculture, forests, water bodies, urban growth, and environmental changes.

Sentinel-2 Processing Levels

Level-0
Raw satellite telemetry data
Level-1A
Radiometric correction applied
Level-1B
Geometric & radiometric correction
Level-1C
Orthorectified TOA reflectance
Level-2A
Surface reflectance (BOA)

How Sentinel-2 Data Is Organized

  • 🧩 Data is divided into UTM tiles, not full scenes
  • 📦 Products follow the SAFE format
  • 📁 Includes GRANULE, DATASTRIP, AUX_DATA, QI_DATA folders
  • 📄 Metadata is stored in XML & manifest.safe
